About Dr. Matthew Doyle

Quantum Physics Researcher & Educator

Academic Background

I completed my PhD in Condensed Matter Physics at The University of Manchester, specialising in quantum turbulence and quantum fluid dynamics. With over 4 years of teaching experience at various higher-education levels, I have developed a deep understanding of how to effectively communicate complex physics concepts to students. My research background, combined with my passion for education, allows me to bring both theoretical depth and practical insights to my teaching.

Teaching Expertise

I offer specialised tutoring across a wide range of physics and mathematics topics, with a teaching approach that focuses on:

  • Breaking down complex concepts into digestible components
  • Connecting theoretical principles to real-world applications
  • Developing problem-solving strategies tailored to each student
  • Building confidence through structured learning paths

Areas of Specialisation

  • A-Level Physics and Mathematics (all exam boards)
  • Undergraduate Physics (all years)
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Mathematical Methods for Physics
  • Classical Mechanics and Electromagnetism

Tutorial Availability

I currently offer both online and in-person tutorials in the Manchester area. Whether you're preparing for examinations, need help with coursework, or want to deepen your understanding of physics, I'm here to help. Contact me to discuss your learning goals and arrange a session.
